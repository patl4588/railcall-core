Before you can enter — three gates, no exceptions

Entries from people who haven't used the product score zero, so we gate the door instead of wasting your time.

GATE 01
Create an account
Sign up at railcall.ai — free tier, no card. Your account email goes on your entry.
GATE 02
Install & build
Run curl -fsSL https://railcall.ai/install.sh | bash, open the Studio, and build at least one real workflow in plain English. Keep the receipt file — it's your entry ticket.
GATE 03
Join the Discord
Join via railcall.ai/discord and say hello in the contest channel. All Q&A, dates, and winner announcements live there.
The receipt is the entry ticket. Every submission must include the signed receipt JSON from a workflow built on your own install. We verify it offline with railcall verify. If the receipt doesn't verify, the entry doesn't count — the same standard we hold ourselves to on the examples page.

Four ways to win

Enter in one category; judges score across all four.

Best Program
The most impressive real workflow or program composed on RailCall — receipt-verified, reproducible, honestly documented.
Best Use Case
The most valuable real-world application: a workflow you (or a business you know) would actually run every week, documented end to end.
Best Feedback
The sharpest reproducible audit: what's broken, confusing, or overclaimed — exact steps, expected vs actual. We pay for bad news.
Material Change
A finding we actually ship. Our last outside auditor's report was fixed and deployed within the hour. If your entry changes the product, that's the strongest entry there is.

What your submission includes

  • Account email — we confirm the signup exists.
  • Discord handle — we confirm you joined.
  • Receipt JSON — from a workflow built on your install; verified offline, non-negotiable.
  • The entry itself — program/use-case write-up, or a numbered findings document.
  • One paragraph: the mission in your own words. If you can't state it, you haven't used it.
