6. Billing, metering & refunds

Prepaid model. Paid flows are sold on a prepaid basis. You purchase a balance of flows, and each governed flow draws down that balance. Your prepaid flow balance does not expire for as long as your account remains in good standing. There are no subscriptions and no per-seat charges; you pay only for the flows you buy.

Blind metering. When the gateway meters a flow, the client transmits only a one-way hash of your API key together with a single-use nonce, solely so the gateway can verify your balance and record the flow. The gateway is a transaction register, not a data sink: it does not receive, and is not designed to receive, your raw API key, your provider keys, your files, your workflow data, or your generated code. Those never leave your machine. You acknowledge that flow counts recorded by the gateway are the authoritative basis for billing.
