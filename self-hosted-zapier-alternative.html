What is Self-Hosted Automation?

Self-hosted automation refers to the practice of running automated workflows on your own machine, rather than relying on cloud-based services. This approach gives you complete control over your data and workflows, ensuring that your sensitive information remains private and secure. With a self-hosted solution, you can automate tasks and workflows without worrying about data breaches or third-party access.

Benefits of Self-Hosted Automation

Self-hosted automation offers several benefits, including increased security, flexibility, and control. By running your automated workflows on your own machine, you can ensure that your data is protected and secure. Additionally, self-hosted automation allows you to customize and extend your workflows to meet your specific needs, without being limited by cloud-based service providers.

How RailCall Works

RailCall is a self-hosted automation platform that allows you to create and run automated workflows on your own machine. With RailCall, you can define your workflows using plain English, and then run them on your local machine. Every run is sealed with an Ed25519 receipt, which can be verified offline, ensuring the integrity and security of your workflows. RailCall also provides a free tier with 500 flows per month, and a flat rate of $0.01 per flow thereafter.
