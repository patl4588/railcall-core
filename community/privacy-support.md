Your data, in support and everywhere else

At RailCall, we're committed to keeping your data exactly where it belongs - on your machine. Our local-first approach ensures that keys, files, workflow data, and generated code never leave your device. This means you're always in control, with no risk of unauthorized access or data breaches.

When it comes to billing, our gateway is designed as a transaction register, not a data sink. We only see a hashed key and a one-time nonce, ensuring your sensitive information remains protected. Every governed flow also generates an Ed25519-signed receipt, which can be verified offline for added security and transparency.

Even our support AI is designed with your data privacy in mind. Unlike industry norms, our API doesn't train on your data by default, and we offer Zero Data Retention. This means your conversations with our support team are never used to improve our models or read by humans. You can trust that your data is handled with the care and respect it deserves. With RailCall, you can focus on building and governing your flows, knowing your data is safe and secure. Verify the receipts, check the hashes - the proof is in the protocol.
