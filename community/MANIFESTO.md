# RailCall Manifesto
We reject the surveillance-AI status quo, where vendors train on our data and read our secrets. We refuse to be mined, monitored, and manipulated. Our code, our keys, our files, and our data are ours alone. We will not be tracked, traced, or tapped. We demand agency, autonomy, and transparency. With RailCall, we own 100% of our code and our destiny. Our gates are guarded, our actions are approved, and our receipts are signed. We verify our freedom offline, with every hash and every seal. We are the Conductors of our own domain, and we will not be derailed. We are the Crew that builds, deploys, and governs with integrity. All aboard for sovereignty, accountability, and trust. Our rails are open, but our data is locked. Join us, and take back control.
